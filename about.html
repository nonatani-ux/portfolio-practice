Our Mission

Educating Hearts & Minds for Life

Founded in 2005, Evergreen Academy has grown into one of the region's most respected independent schools. Our approach blends academic rigour with a deep commitment to personal development, wellbeing and community.

We believe every child is capable of extraordinary things — our role is to provide the environment, mentorship and opportunities that help them find out.

  • Accredited Cambridge & IB curriculum delivery
  • Average class size of 18 students for personal attention
  • Inclusive, diverse and welcoming school community
  • State-of-the-art STEM labs and performing arts centre
  • Strong alumni network spanning 40+ countries
